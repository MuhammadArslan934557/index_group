Our Mission

Index Group is a dynamic consortium of companies dedicated to real estate development with its headquarters in Pakistan and Kingdom With a collective expertise and experience in the industry, we specialize in transforming land and properties into thriving residential, commercial and mixed-use spaces.

Real estate development is our core focus, where we strive to create innovative, sustainable and engaging residential, commercial, and mixed-use spaces. From the initial stages of planning and design to the execution of construction and marketing strategies, Our comprehensive services are tailored to meet the unique requirements of each project. Beyond the realm of real estate, we leverage our expertise in various major industries such as hospitality, retail, healthcare, education, and more. By understanding the specific needs and challenges of each industry, we provide tailored solutions that deliver exceptional results, solidifying our position as a versatile consortium.
